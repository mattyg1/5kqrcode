About 5K QR Code

Built by parkrunners, for parkrunners.

Our Story

5K QR Code was born from a simple need: making parkrun more convenient and accessible for everyone. As regular parkrunners ourselves, we experienced the common frustrations of forgotten barcodes, damaged paper cards, and the inconvenience of carrying physical tokens.

The idea came to us during a rainy Saturday morning at our local parkrun. We watched fellow runners searching through wet pockets for soggy barcodes, and we thought: "There has to be a better way."

What started as a weekend project quickly evolved into a comprehensive parkrun companion app, designed to enhance the experience for the entire parkrun community.

Our Mission

We believe that parkrun should be accessible, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone. Our mission is to:

  • Simplify parkrun participation by providing reliable, digital alternatives to physical barcodes
  • Enhance the parkrun experience with beautiful visualizations and progress tracking
  • Protect privacy by keeping all data local and secure on your device
  • Support the community by creating tools that work for individuals, families, and friends
  • Promote accessibility by ensuring our app works for parkrunners of all abilities

Our Values

Privacy First

We believe your data belongs to you. That's why 5K QR Code stores everything locally on your device. No tracking, no data collection, no external servers. Your parkrun journey is yours alone.

Community Focused

parkrun is about community, and so are we. We've designed our app to support families, friends, and running groups who participate together. Everyone deserves to track their progress and celebrate their achievements.

Simplicity & Reliability

Race day should be stress-free. Our app is designed to be simple, reliable, and work perfectly when you need it most ‚Äì even without an internet connection.

Continuous Improvement

We're constantly listening to the parkrun community and improving our app based on real user feedback. Every update is designed to make your parkrun experience better.

How We Built It

Technology Stack

5K QR Code is built using the latest Apple technologies:

  • SwiftUI for modern, responsive user interfaces
  • SwiftData for secure, local data persistence
  • WatchConnectivity for seamless iPhone and Apple Watch integration
  • Core Image for high-quality QR code and barcode generation
  • UserNotifications for helpful parkrun reminders

Design Philosophy

We've followed Apple's Human Interface Guidelines to create an app that feels natural and intuitive. Every interaction is designed to be clear, efficient, and delightful.

Testing & Quality

Before every release, we thoroughly test the app across multiple devices and iOS versions. We also test at real parkrun events to ensure everything works perfectly on race day.
